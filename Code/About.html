Brighton Jones - Winter 2020 Tax Intern

During January 2020 to May 2020, I was given an extraordinary opportunity to spend my time at the #1 Financial
Advisor firm in Seattle, Brighton Jones. As a tax intern, I received invaluable opportunities to not only learn about the
various tax forms (such as 1040, 1041, 1065, 1120s) but also to experience what it is like to work in a highly-driven,
collaborative, and professional environment. Additionally, I was able to make connections with the nicest and most
genuine people I have ever met.

Since the first moment to the end, everything from people to education has been amazing. The connections I made at
Brighton Jones didn’t seem as though I was just an intern but as an actual part of the team. My mentors and
supervisors really spent time to make sure that my colleagues and I have our voice heard and made sure we were
given the best learning possibility possible.

What spoke out to me greatly about my time as a tax intern is everyone at Brighton Jones cares about each other very
much. Quoting from one of my mentors which has spoke true about Brighton Jones and the wonderful tax team
throughout, “When you’re here, you’re family.” Everyone knows your name and the culture made me feel like it is my second
home.

All in all, if I were to go back in time and received the opportunity to intern at Brighton Jones, I would take it in a
heartbeat.
